## Score scale

- **Likelihood**: Low (1) / Medium (2) / High (3)
- **Impact**: Low (1) / Medium (2) / High (3)
- **Score = Likelihood × Impact** (range 1-9)
- **Action threshold**:
  - Score 6-9: 🔴 Active mitigation required; review weekly
  - Score 3-5: 🟡 Track; review monthly
  - Score 1-2: ⚪ Document; review quarterly

## How to add a risk

1. Increment to next R<N> ID
2. Score Likelihood × Impact
3. Identify owner (must be a real person, not "the team")
4. Mitigation must be concrete: a specific decision, runbook, deliverable, or action — not just "watch out for this"
5. Status: 🔴 Open / 🟡 Open / ⚪ Open / ⚫ Closed

## How risks close

- Mitigation lands and reduces score below 3 → close
- Risk no longer applies (e.g., scope change) → close
- Risk materialized → keep open as "🔴 Materialized" until incident response complete; then close as "Materialized — see post-mortem"

When closing: append to "Closed" section below. Never delete; append-only audit trail.
